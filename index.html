What do we provide do?

Our goal at Union Shield Security is to set new standards in safeguarding by delivering unwavering protection and innovative security solutions that inspire trust and empower peace of mind for our clients across the United Kingdom.

Access Control and Surveillance:

Our security firm specializes in implementing robust access control and surveillance solutions to ensure the safety and integrity of your premises. We offer state-of-the-art technology that effectively manages and monitors entry points, visitor registration, and employee access, helping you prevent unauthorized entry and monitor activities in real-time. With our comprehensive surveillance systems, you'll have eyes on critical areas, day and night, providing a secure environment and swift response to any potential security breaches.

Mobile Patrol and Alarm Response:

At Union Shield Security, we understand the importance of a proactive approach to security. Our dedicated mobile patrol teams are trained to conduct regular checks of your property, deterring criminal activities and addressing any vulnerabilities. With our swift alarm response services, you can rest assured that any triggered alarms will be met with a rapid and efficient reaction from our trained professionals. Whether it's an emergency situation or a routine patrol, our mobile teams are always ready to safeguard your premises and maintain peace of mind.

Risk Assessment and Consultation:

Tailoring our services to your unique needs, we provide expert risk assessment and consultation services to help you identify potential security gaps and develop a comprehensive security strategy. Our experienced consultants collaborate closely with you to evaluate your premises, operations, and specific concerns, enabling us to recommend a range of practical measures to enhance security. From security system upgrades to procedural enhancements, our personalized consultation ensures that your security measures are well-aligned with your objectives and provide optimal protection.
